IDP3 program

The Interactive Media Design program (IDP3) at Fanshawe's Downtown campus blends creativity and technology with an emphasis on hands-on learning. Students take on projects in areas such as Graphic Design, 3D Modeling, Front-end Development, Back-end Development, Animation and Interactive Media. Gaining practical experience in both creative and technical aspects is key to their success.

Graduates leave with a professional portfolio that showcasing their range and growth, opening pathways to careers in digital design, web and application devlopment, media production, and related fields. The program not only develops technical expertise but also fosters adaptability, preparing students to succeed in am industry that is always evolving.
